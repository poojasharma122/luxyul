Events Calendar

The Principality’s Most Celebrated Moments, All Year Round

From high-speed racing to world-class yachting and cultural festivals, Monaco’s calendar is packed with events that shape the Riviera’s identity. Whether you plan your visit around a major spectacle or stumble upon a local celebration, these occasions offer a deeper connection to life in the principality.

January

International Circus Festival of Monte-Carlo

A showcase of acrobatic brilliance, animal performances, and world-class acts, founded by Prince Rainier III in 1974. It attracts performers from across the globe and fills the Chapiteau de Fontvieille with colour and excitement.

  • Duration: Mid-to-late January
  • Venue: Chapiteau de Fontvieille
  • Insider Tip: Book tickets early for prime seating during the award ceremonies.
April

Monte-Carlo Rolex Masters

One of the most prestigious tournaments on the ATP Tour, played on the red clay courts overlooking the Mediterranean. A perfect mix of sport and glamour.

  • Duration: Mid-April
  • Venue: Monte-Carlo Country Club
  • Insider Tip: Stay nearby for easy access and the chance to spot top players around town.
May

Monaco Grand Prix

The most famous street race in the world. Formula 1 cars speed through tight corners and along the harbourfront, with grandstands, private terraces, and yachts offering different vantage points.

  • Duration: Usually the last weekend of May
  • Venue: Circuit de Monaco (throughout Monte Carlo)
  • Insider Tip: Secure hotel rooms and race packages months in advance; demand is unmatched.
June

Monte-Carlo Television Festival

Celebrating excellence in international television, with screenings, panel discussions, and celebrity appearances.

  • Duration: Mid-June
  • Venue: Grimaldi Forum
  • Insider Tip: Some red-carpet events are open to the public — a great chance to see stars up close.
July

Monte-Carlo Sporting Summer Festival

A series of live music performances and gala dinners featuring international artists, staged under the stars in an intimate setting.

  • Duration: July–August
  • Venue: Salle des Étoiles, Sporting Monte-Carlo
  • Insider Tip: Book a dinner show for the full experience.
September

Monaco Yacht Show

The ultimate yachting event, with the world’s most spectacular superyachts on display. It draws industry professionals, designers, and buyers from across the globe.

  • Duration: Late September
  • Venue: Port Hercule
  • Insider Tip: Arrange a private yacht viewing through a local broker for an insider’s look.
October

Monte-Carlo Jazz Festival

A celebration of jazz music in the elegant setting of the Opéra Garnier, featuring a blend of legendary musicians and rising stars.

  • Duration: Late October–November
  • Venue: Opéra Garnier
  • Insider Tip: Combine your visit with a pre-show dinner in the Carré d’Or district.
December

Monaco Christmas Village

Port Hercule transforms into a festive market with themed chalets, rides, and seasonal performances.

  • Duration: Early December to early January
  • Venue: Quai Albert Ier, Port Hercule
  • Insider Tip: Visit in the evening for the full effect of lights and live music.

Planning Your Trip Around Monaco Events

Booking early is key for major events like the Grand Prix, Rolex Masters, and Yacht Show.

Smaller cultural festivals and seasonal celebrations offer more flexibility but can still impact hotel availability.
