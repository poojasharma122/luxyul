Stay Collection

The Finest Stays in Monaco & Monte Carlo

In a destination where every property makes a statement, Luxyul presents a curated selection of Monaco’s most exceptional hotels and residences. Each offers its own take on Riviera elegance, from heritage icons to modern retreats.

Hôtel de Paris Monte-Carlo

From €650 per night

Monaco’s most celebrated address, standing proudly over Place du Casino.

  • Michelin-starred dining, including Le Louis XV – Alain Ducasse
  • Direct access to Thermes Marins Monte-Carlo
  • One of the largest wine cellars in the world

Since opening in 1864, Hôtel de Paris has set the gold standard for Riviera luxury. Its Belle Époque façade and grand lobby lead to sumptuously appointed rooms, many overlooking the harbour or casino square. Dining is a highlight, with Le Louis XV holding three Michelin stars, and guests can enjoy direct spa and wellness access without leaving the property. The atmosphere is one of timeless prestige, attracting royalty, celebrities, and discerning travellers.

Hôtel Hermitage Monte-Carlo

From €480 per night

Refined elegance and sweeping harbour views in the heart of Monte Carlo.

  • Eiffel-designed glass-domed winter garden
  • Exclusive Monte-Carlo Beach Club access
  • Central yet tranquil setting

The Hôtel Hermitage is an exercise in understated luxury, where Belle Époque architecture meets modern comfort. Its Winter Garden, designed by Gustave Eiffel, bathes the hotel in natural light and serves as a serene focal point. Rooms and suites are decorated in soft tones, offering harbour or courtyard views. Service is warm yet discreet, and the location places guests steps from both the casino and the waterfront.

Monte-Carlo Bay Hotel & Resort

From €350 per night

A resort-style escape with gardens, lagoon, and seafront views.

  • Sandy-bottom lagoon pool
  • Michelin-starred Blue Bay restaurant
  • Expansive gardens with Mediterranean landscaping

Situated on the Larvotto seafront, Monte-Carlo Bay Hotel & Resort combines leisure and sophistication. Guests can lounge in the lagoon-style pool, stroll landscaped gardens, or dine at the award-winning Blue Bay. Rooms are contemporary with warm Riviera touches, many offering balconies over the sea. This is a property that suits both couples seeking romance and families looking for a full resort experience within Monaco.

Fairmont Monte Carlo

From €320 per night

An urban resort at the edge of the Mediterranean and Grand Prix track.

  • Rooftop pool with panoramic sea views
  • Nobu restaurant on-site
  • Overlooks the iconic Fairmont Hairpin bend

The Fairmont occupies one of Monaco’s most recognisable locations, with the Mediterranean on one side and the Monaco Grand Prix hairpin turn on the other. Rooms are spacious and designed for comfort, with balconies framing sea or city views. The rooftop pool offers a prime spot for sunbathing, while Nobu delivers world-class dining without leaving the hotel. Popular with Grand Prix guests and those wanting a central base with resort amenities.

Le Méridien Beach Plaza

From €290 per night

Modern style and Monaco’s only private beach.

  • Exclusive private beachfront
  • Floor-to-ceiling sea views
  • Close to Grimaldi Forum

Le Méridien Beach Plaza offers a rare beachfront location in Monaco. Its contemporary rooms and suites maximise views through large glass windows, while the private beach club is ideal for daytime relaxation. The hotel’s location near the Grimaldi Forum makes it a strong choice for both business and leisure travellers.

Columbus Monte-Carlo

From €220 per night

Relaxed Riviera style in the quiet Fontvieille district.

  • Overlooks Princess Grace Rose Garden
  • Close to Fontvieille harbour
  • Chic, modern interiors

For those seeking a quieter side of Monaco, Columbus Monte-Carlo delivers. Set in the charming Fontvieille area, it’s away from the bustle but still within easy reach of the principality’s highlights. Rooms feature clean lines and a subtle coastal palette. Ideal for repeat visitors or those who prefer a boutique atmosphere.

Port Palace Hotel

From €250 per night

Boutique harbourfront property with all-suite accommodation.

  • All rooms offer Port Hercule views
  • Steps from the Yacht Club
  • Intimate, personalised service

Port Palace is a hidden gem overlooking Monaco’s busy harbour. Its 50 suites are spacious, with floor-to-ceiling windows framing the luxury yachts moored below. The hotel’s small scale allows for attentive service, and the location is unbeatable for yacht show visitors and Grand Prix fans.

Hotel Metropole Monte-Carlo

From €450 per night

Glamour and design in the heart of Monte Carlo.

  • Interiors by Jacques Garcia
  • Odyssey pool area by Karl Lagerfeld
  • Two Michelin-starred restaurants

Hotel Metropole blends Mediterranean glamour with world-class design. The Odyssey pool area is a standout, combining Karl Lagerfeld’s artistic touch with exceptional comfort. Michelin dining and opulent interiors make it a favourite for guests who want style and substance in equal measure.

Top-floor luxury with panoramic Riviera views.

  • Wraparound terrace
