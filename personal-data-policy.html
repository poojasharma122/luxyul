Personal Data Policy

At Luxyul (“we,” “our,” “us”), we take the protection of your personal data seriously. This Personal Data Policy explains how we handle, safeguard, and process personal information collected through luxyul.com (the “Site”) or via direct communication with you.

1. Our Commitment to Data Protection

We are committed to:

  • Collecting only the personal data necessary for legitimate purposes.
  • Processing personal data lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
  • Keeping personal data accurate and up to date.
  • Protecting personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, loss, or disclosure.
  • Never selling, renting, or trading your personal information to third parties.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Identification Data – Name, email address, and any details provided in correspondence
  • Technical Data – IP address, browser type, operating system, and device information.
  • Usage Data – Pages visited, time spent on the Site, navigation paths, and interaction details.

3. How We Use Your Data

Personal data is used solely for purposes such as:

  • Responding to your enquiries and providing requested information.
  • Improving the quality, accuracy, and relevance of our content.
  • Sending newsletters or updates (only with your explicit consent).
  • Complying with legal or regulatory obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data on one or more of the following grounds:

  • Consent – When you opt in to communications or provide information voluntarily.
  • Legitimate Interests – For site optimisation, analytics, and security measures.
  • Legal Obligations – Where required by applicable law or in response to lawful requests.

5. Data Sharing

We will not share personal data except in the following circumstances:

  • With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our Site and are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • When required by applicable laws, regulations, or legal proceedings.

6. Data Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law. Data that is no longer needed will be securely deleted or anonymised.

7. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, including the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request corrections or updates to your information.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Restrict or object to certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

Requests can be made by contacting us at info@luxyul.com.

8. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organisational safeguards to protect your personal data. While we take all practical precautions, no system can be guaranteed to be entirely secure.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Personal Data Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Personal Data Policy or how we handle personal data, contact us at:

Email: info@luxyul.com
