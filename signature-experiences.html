Signature Experiences

Monaco & Monte Carlo Beyond the Hotel

From world-famous motorsport to private yacht charters, Monaco offers experiences that define the Riviera lifestyle. Luxyul curates activities that combine exclusivity, access, and unforgettable settings — each one selected to complement your stay in the principality.

Monaco Grand Prix Weekend Package

From €4,800 per person

The ultimate way to experience Formula 1 in the streets of Monte Carlo.

  • Trackside VIP terrace seating
  • Exclusive hospitality suite with gourmet catering
  • Access to post-race celebrations

This package delivers the thrill of Formula 1 from the most coveted vantage points. Guests watch the race from private balconies or rooftop terraces overlooking the track, enjoying uninterrupted views of the cars as they navigate the city streets. Premium catering, open bars, and live entertainment make it a once-in-a-lifetime weekend for motorsport enthusiasts.

Private Yacht Charter

From €3,200 per day

Sail the Riviera coastline aboard a fully crewed luxury yacht.

  • Customisable itineraries along the French and Italian coasts
  • Gourmet onboard dining and bar service
  • Swim stops in secluded bays

Departing from Port Hercule, this charter experience combines Monaco’s maritime elegance with the freedom to explore at your own pace. Crews are experienced in providing discreet, attentive service, ensuring each day on the water is tailored to your preferences. Perfect for celebrations or tranquil escapes.

Monte Carlo Casino Experience

From €450 per person

An evening inside Monaco’s most famous gaming rooms.

  • Guided introduction to the Casino de Monte-Carlo’s history
  • Reserved gaming table with private croupier
  • Champagne and fine dining included

The Casino de Monte-Carlo is more than a gaming destination — it’s a cultural landmark. This hosted evening blends history and high stakes, beginning with a private tour before guests settle in for an exclusive gaming session. Ideal for both seasoned players and those looking to try their luck in style.

Helicopter Transfer & Riviera Tour

From €720 per person

Arrive in Monaco in style and see the Côte d’Azur from above.

  • Scenic 7-minute flight from Nice Airport
  • Optional extended coastal tour
  • Priority boarding and luggage handling

Monaco is just minutes from Nice by air, and a helicopter transfer offers the fastest, most scenic arrival possible. Extend your flight for a full Riviera aerial tour, taking in Saint-Tropez, Cannes, and the Italian border. An unforgettable start or end to your Monaco visit.

Fine Dining Experience at Le Louis XV – Alain Ducasse

From €380 per person

A culinary journey in one of Europe’s most celebrated restaurants.

  • Multi-course tasting menu
  • Wine pairing from a legendary cellar
  • Exclusive kitchen visit available on request

Dining at Le Louis XV is an experience in itself, with Mediterranean flavours refined to perfection. The setting within Hôtel de Paris adds a sense of grandeur, while the service is meticulously choreographed. Perfect for marking a special occasion during your Monaco stay.

Monaco Harbour Sunset Cruise

From €950 per couple

A private, champagne-filled evening on the water.

  • Depart from Port Hercule at golden hour
  • Personal skipper and onboard champagne service
  • Views of the illuminated coastline and royal palace

The principality is stunning by day but truly magical at sunset. This cruise allows guests to take in the glittering skyline and serene waters in complete privacy. The perfect romantic end to a day in Monaco.

Behind-the-Scenes at the Oceanographic Museum

From €260 per person

An after-hours tour of one of Monaco’s cultural treasures.

  • Private guide and museum curator
  • Access to research areas and aquarium labs
  • Wine and canapé reception in the museum’s rooftop terrace

This exclusive evening reveals the Oceanographic Museum’s hidden corners. Learn about ongoing marine research, meet aquarium experts, and enjoy refreshments while overlooking the Mediterranean from the rooftop. A must for culture and science enthusiasts.
